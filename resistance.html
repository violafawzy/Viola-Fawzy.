What is Bacteria resistance?!

Defination:

Bacteria resistance is the capacity of bacteria to withstand the effects of antibiotics or biocides that are intended to kill or control them.

Types:

There is three types of resistance

susceptible
intermediate
resistant

Steps:

There is four steps

  • uptake of a drug.

  • modifying a drug target.

  • inactivating a drug.

  • active drug efflux.
